Lior Milliger - DevOps Engineer

My journey into DevOps started with a simple realization: computers never forget what you tell them to do,
they just do it exactly as you said (which, as it turns out, isn’t always what you meant).
That curiosity for how systems behave, and occasionally misbehave, led me to what I do today:
building scalable, reliable, and secure infrastructure that behaves exactly as intended.


Over the years, I’ve worked across cloud platforms, CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes clusters, and monitoring stacks,
designing systems that make both developers and operations teams sleep better at night (most nights, anyway).
I believe great DevOps isn’t just about automation, it’s about understanding how people and systems interact,
and removing friction between the two.
